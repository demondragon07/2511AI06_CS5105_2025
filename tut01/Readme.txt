Step1--
open the below link
https://studentgroupmaker.streamlit.app/

Step2--
In the input section, upload your student data file (.xlsx or .csv).

Step3-:
Enter the Number of Groups you want to create.

Step4--:
Click on the Generate Groups button.

Step5--
After generation, you will see 4 options. You can explore each of them:
1)Full Branchwise – Shows students of each branch separately.
2)Group Branchwise Mix – Groups created with branchwise proportional mixing.
3)Uniform Mix – Groups created with uniform distribution of students.
4)Summary Tables – Overview of group sizes, branch distribution matrix, and statistics.

Step6--
Click on Download All Files (ZIP) to download all generated group files at once.
